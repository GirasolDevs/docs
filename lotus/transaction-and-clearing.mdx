## Transaction and clearing

The transaction and clearing process is a critical component of the payment system. It involves transferring funds from the customer's account to the merchant's account. This process begins when the customer initiates a transaction, which is then processed by the bank. Once the transaction is authorized, the bank transfers the funds to the merchant's account, completing the transaction.
